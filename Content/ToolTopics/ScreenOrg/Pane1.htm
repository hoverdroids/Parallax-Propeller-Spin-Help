Pane 1: the Object View pane.  The Propeller chip’s language, Spin, is object-based and a Propeller Project can be made up of multiple objects.  The Object View displays the hierarchical view of the objects in the project you most recently compiled successfully, providing valuable feedback on the relational structure of your project.  Using the Object View, you can determine what objects are used, how they fit together with other objects, their physical location on disk, redundancy optimization results (if any) and any potential object collision issues.
