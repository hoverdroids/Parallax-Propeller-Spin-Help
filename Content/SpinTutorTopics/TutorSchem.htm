Schematics for Spin Tutorial

The Spin Programming Tutorial relies heavily on circuitry similar to that of the Propeller Demo Board. You may use a different Propeller development board and the Propeller Tutorial Schematics below. An RCA-to-Breadboard Adapter part# 28050 is available from www.parallax.com.

 

Propeller Tutorial Schematics

 

If you would like to build your own Propeller programming circuit, you may use one of the schematics below, and refer to the Package & Pin Descriptions as well.

Typical Hardware Connection (with Propeller Plug USB-to-Serial adapter)

Example wiring diagram that allows for programming the Propeller chip and an external 32 Kbyte EEPROM, and running the Propeller with an external crystal.  The Propeller Plug is part# 32201; available from www.parallax.com.

 

Alternative Hardware Connection (with standard DB-9 serial port)

Alternative example for programming the Propeller chip without the Propeller Plug. Propeller chip not shown.   

 

You may also refer to the schematic of the Propeller Demo Board.
