Objects vs. Cogs

It's important to understand that there is no direct relationship between objects and cogs. Remember, Exercise 4-2 used just one object but two cogs, and Exercise 5-1 used two objects and two cogs. However, each of those exercises could have used only one cog if we had wanted to process everything serially. When and how cogs are used is completely determined by the application and the developer who wrote it.
