Questions and Answers

 

As you learn more about the Propeller, you are bound to have some fundamental questions for which you can not easily find the answers. This documentation is meant to help with those fundamental issues and may also answer some questions you never thought to ask.

This is a collection of dozens of questions and their corresponding answers arranged into categories, listed in the Table of Contents (and below). We recommend that beginners read the first few categories in order, and users with more experience skip around as needed.
